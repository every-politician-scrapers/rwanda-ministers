The Cabinet

The Cabinet is composed of the Prime Minister, Ministers, Ministers of State and other members who may be determined by the President of the Republic where deemed necessary. The Cabinet implements national policies agreed upon by the President of the Republic and the Cabinet meeting. It is accountable to the President of the Republic and the Parliament.

THE PRIME MINISTER'S OFFICE

Visit website →

The Prime Minister’s Office, under the authority of the Prime Minister, is entrusted with assisting the Prime Minister in the implementation of national policies, leading Government activities along the major guidelines given by the President of the Republic and ensuring law enforcement. The PMO is also entrusted with the function of formulating the Government programme of action in consultation with Ministerial Departments.

The Ministry of Finance and Economic Planning (MINECOFIN) has a mission to raise sustainable growth, economic opportunities, and living standards of all Rwandans.
